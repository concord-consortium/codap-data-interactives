An Exploration of Sampling

At left is a sampler. Press its Run button to see it work.

It mixes the labeled balls and chooses or draws, one at a time at random. After the ball's label is recorded, the ball returns to the mix.

After the sampler has drawn two balls, it puts the results in a data set. You can see a table of the data by pressing this button:

Press the Run button again and watch the draws added to the table.

Notice that each time you press Run, you get a new experiment. And each experiment has as many runs as there are repetitions.

The rightmost portion of the table keeps track of the draws.
