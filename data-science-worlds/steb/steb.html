The Basics

Press new game to begin.

You are a predator. Eat the circles. They're called "Stebbers."

Do NOT eat the "Crud." The Crud look a lot like Stebbers, but they're NOT STEBBERS.

Whenever you eat a Stebber, a new one is born from a randomly-chosen "parent."

Data are collected every ten meals, or whenever you press data now.

Options

In the options tab, you can change the colors of the background and the crud. The Red, Green, and Blue amounts are numbers between 0 and 15. So for example, [3, 3, 12] is pretty blue.

Evolution simulation about protective coloration. You are the predator.

Based on the old, offline "Stebbins Game,” created by the great evolutionary biologist, G. Ledyard Stebbins.
